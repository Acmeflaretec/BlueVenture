Universities

Top Universities in Georgia

University of Georgia

UG is a private University that was founded in 2004 with hundreds of international students and international visiting scholars. UG also provides Engineering, MBA, BBA, and nursing programs for international students.

Tbilisi State Medical University (TSMU)

TSMU is the largest and leading Government Medical University in Georgia and Eastern Europe. There are almost 7500 undergraduates and 3000 postgraduate students in the university from which 25% come from foreign countries and have produced more than 40000 Doctors.

Ivane Javakhishvili Tbilisi State University

The First-ever Government Research University in the Caucasus, established in 1918 laying the foundation for a European type higher school in Georgia. Ranked amongst one of the top 1000 universities of the world

Tbilisi Medical Academy (TMA)

TMA is a Georgian private Medical University which was founded in 1992. It is one of the oldest private universities in the region with an excellent student-teacher ratio.

East European University

East European University (EEU) is one of the best private medical university in Tbilisi, Georgia. In 2012 EEU obtained authorization from the Ministry of education and Science of Georgia .

David Tvildiani Medical University (DTMU)

DTMU is the first Non-Governmental Medical University in Georgia which was established in 1989. The University provides high quality scientifically – oriented medical education that meets international benchmarks.
