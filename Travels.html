Mother of Georgia

Mother of Georgia symbolizes the Georgian national character: in her left hand she holds a bowl of wine to greet those who come as friends, and in her right hand is a sword for those who come as enemies as expression of the history of our city of Tbilisi

Panorama Gudauri

The Russia–Georgia Friendship Monument a monument built in 1983. the ongoing friendship between Soviet Georgia and Soviet Russia.

Canyon

Traverse through the mesmerizing canyons, each with its distinct beauty and geological features.

Ananuri Fortress

A medieval defensive fortress on the Georgian Military Road. Ananuri is a castle complex on the Aragvi River in Dusheti Municipality Georgia

Chronicle of Georgia

Discover the monumental depiction of Georgian history through intricate stone carvings and sculptures.

Gergeti Trinity Church

Experience the awe-inspiring presence of the medieval church against the backdrop of the Caucasus Mountains.

Black and White Aragvi River

The Aragvi River(Black & White aragvi) of Two Colors Viewpoint marks the meeting point of two rivers the Black and White Aragvi. It flows from Gudauri down to the town of Pasanauri.

Bridge of Peace

Walk across the stunning modern pedestrian bridge that connects Tbilisi’s old and new districts.
